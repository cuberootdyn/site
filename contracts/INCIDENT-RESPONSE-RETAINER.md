## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Incident Response services include:
- **Triage:** Rapid assessment of the situation and containment strategies.
- **Forensics:** Deep-dive analysis of logs, disk images, and memory to identify root cause.
- **Containment & Eradication:** Assistance in removing the threat and preventing lateral movement.
- **Recovery:** Guidance on restoring systems and data from backups.
- **Post-Incident Review:** Final report including a timeline of events and recommendations.

## 3. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
